Welcome To Mahakumbh-2025

The Maha Kumbh Mela 2025 is a significant Hindu pilgrimage and festival that will be held in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, from January 13 to February 26
It's the world's largest peaceful gathering, drawing millions of devotees and tourists to bathe in the sacred rivers and participate in rituals focused on spiritual purification and enlightenment.
